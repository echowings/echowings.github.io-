Ubuntu Chinese Language Support

  • 编辑/etc/environment如下:

    1

    vi /etc/environment

添加如下:

1
2
3
4

LC_ALL="en_US.UTF-8"
LANG="zh_CN.UTF-8"
UNZIP='-O CP936"
ZIPINFO="-O CP936"

  • 重新生成语言设置

    1
2

     sudo locale-gen  en_US en_US.UTF-8 zh_CN zh_CN.UTF-8
